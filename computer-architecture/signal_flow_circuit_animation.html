Setup Time (t_setup)

Data D must be stable before the clock edge.

Hold Time (t_hold)

Data D must remain stable after the clock edge.

FF Prop Delay (t_ffpd)

Time for DFF internal state to reach output Q.

Comb. Delay (t_comb)

Time for signals to traverse logic gates.

Clock Period Constraint (T_clk)

To ensure correct operation, the clock period must be long enough for the signal to propagate through the entire loop and meet the setup time requirement of the next cycle.

T_clk ≥ t_ffpd + t_comb + t_setup

Note: If T_clk is shorter than this sum, the DFF will sample unstable data, leading to meta-stability or logic errors.
